# Vrinda Store Sales Analysis Dashboard (2022)
## Objective
To analyze Vrinda Store’s 2022 sales data by creating interactive dashboards and tables in Excel, enabling data-driven insights through structured data processing, cleaning, and visualization.
**Dataset:** Raw data will be uploaded soon. The link will be added here.


## Questions
Compare sales and orders using a single chart.


Which month recorded the highest sales and orders?


Who made more purchases in 2022 — men or women?


What were the different order statuses in 2022?


List the top 5 states contributing to sales.


What is the relationship between age and gender based on the number of orders?


Which sales channel contributed the most to overall sales?


Which product category had the highest sales?

## process
1. Verified the Dataset

Checked the dataset thoroughly to ensure there were no missing values, errors, or anomalies before starting the analysis.

2. Cleaned and Prepared the Data

Performed data cleaning, corrected data formats, standardized values, and ensured the dataset was ready for accurate analysis.

3. Created Pivot Tables

Built multiple pivot tables based on the questions provided, covering sales trends, customer demographics, order status, and other insights.

4. Designed the Dashboard

Merged all pivot tables into a single interactive dashboard and applied slicers to enable dynamic filtering and user-friendly analysis.

## Project Insights

Women are more likely to make purchases compared to men (~65%).

Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top three states contributing to sales (~35%).

The adult age group (30–49 years) contributes the highest share (~50%).

Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the top-performing sales channels (~80%).

## Final Recommendation to Improve Vrinda Store Sales

Focus on targeting women aged 30–49 years.

Prioritize customers living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.

Use ads, offers, and coupons on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra to attract and convert these customers more effectively.
